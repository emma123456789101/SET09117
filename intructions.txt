here you will find instructions on how to play the sudoku game that has been created

1. you must first say how many clues you would like <=20 is hard,>=20 is medium and >=30 is easy.
2. a statement will be printed of what level of difficulty you have chose,this allows the user to easily identify if they have chose the right numbers of clues
3. the inital board of the difficulty level will be chosen
4. then it will ask you to fill in values this includes the values that are on the itial board
5. once that is completed the board will be reprinted will all the new values
